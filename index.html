Be a part of Hacktitude!

‘Hacktitude’ attempts to push university students to enhance their contributions to the open source community. So grab this once in a lifetime opportunity to contribute to the 99X open source repositories and based on your contributions win the chance to work one-on-one with some of the top industry professionals at 99X Technology.

Initial Round

The initial phase of Hacktitude includes all you willing and interested university students contributing to the 99X open source repositories. Based on all your hard work and contributions, the top 10 students who have contributed the most would be selected for the next phase.

Idea selection and mentor pairing

Each of the top 10 contributors from the initial phase will then be individually mentored by one of 99X Technology’s senior professionals to work on their own ideas and create their own repositories based on such ideas.

Final Round

On completion of the idea generation and mentor pairing phase, these finalists would be given two weeks to work on their repositories under the guidance of their specific mentors. At the end of these two weeks, their repositories would be reviewed and the top 3 contributors would be selected and rewarded with prizes!
