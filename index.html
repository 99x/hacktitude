Be a part of Hacktitude!

Make the most of a once in a lifetime opportunity through ‘BeThem’, a unique programme spearheaded by 99X Technology to give you the chance to mingle and work with top industry professionals and gain one-to-one career guidance that will aid you in your future endeavours. This initiative has been launched with the objective of giving undergraduates firsthand exposure to the Software Engineering industry.

Initial Round

The first phase of BeThem industry challenge compose of an online problem solving competition where your problem solving skills will be put to the test through a series of questions. The top 30 contestants will be selected to take part in our main event - BeThem full day interactive program at 99X Technology.

Idea selection and mentor pairing

You will be guided by a team of professionals at this one day program that will expose you into software industry where you could demonstrate your analytical, teamwork, presentation and technical skills. Your ability to step into the shoes of a Software Engineer and perform as one will be key to winning the competition.

Final Round

You will be guided by a team of professionals at this one day program that will expose you into software industry where you could demonstrate your analytical, teamwork, presentation and technical skills. Your ability to step into the shoes of a Software Engineer and perform as one will be key to winning the competition.
